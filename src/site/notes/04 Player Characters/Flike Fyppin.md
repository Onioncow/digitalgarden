## Description
A 7ft tall [[02 Lore/Bestiary/Tanuki\|Mapach]] with dark fur and a crippling gambling addiction. He has a rather cool looking wizards hat.


## History
Born 17th Marpenoth 1481 (absolute) of the 2nd Era in his home town just east of [[05 World/Material Plane/03 Cities/Gefestmere/Gefestmere\|Gefestmere]].

- From the forest near [[05 World/Material Plane/03 Cities/Gefestmere/Gefestmere\|Gefestmere]]. His people didn't agree with gambling so he left home at the age of 8
- Once he left, he joined a church (cult) that worships [[02 Lore/Gods/Outer Gods/The Great Wheel\|The Great Wheel]] as a god of chance and gambling
- He learnt everything he knows about his religion (cult) from Habby
- He has an uncle in the [[05 World/Material Plane/01 Geography/Land/Condeus#Perfumed Quag\|Perfumed Quag]]

### [[01 Campaigns/Hidden Ritual/Hidden Ritual\|Hidden Ritual]]
On his pilgrimage to the secret Casino of [[05 World/Material Plane/03 Cities/Gefestmere/Gefestmere\|Gefestmere]], Flike was unlucky enough to be dragged into a secret time altering ritual. With the help of husbands and friends(?), the ritual was thwarted and the city and lives of those in the ritual were saved.

With a new found talent for reading and writing as bestowed by the magic wheel, flike begins a journey of the scholarly arts. JK, he writes a 981,000 word memoir called: "The [[02 Lore/Bestiary/Tanuki\|Mapach]] and the wheel of indeterminate origin that is of both indistinct form and alignment; considering the effects of early age gambling talent when applied to eldritch energies beyond our plane of existence, the extents of mortal knowledge and the limits of a feeble mammalian mind. Part 1 of 9. Flike and [[04 Player Characters/Companions/Noodle\|noodle]] begin to move around from Casino to casino amassing incalculable wealth with the help of flike's clear gambling talent and [[04 Player Characters/Companions/Noodle\|noodle]]'s unwavering support and affection. Flike marries 8 additional men, adding them to the polycule, and will frequently offer a place in the polycule up as his bet when he runs out of money, so far, no one has taken him up on this because apparently, it's not particularly desirable. Flike keeps in contact with and tries his best to be surrounded by his friends and lovers like Bayle Fyppin the dread, [[03 NPCs Assorted/Owen Wilson\|Owen Wilson]], and the face of Massimo flike cut off and placed on the body of a mannequin made of twigs, leaves and vines. The last line of book 4 - the one most recently released by the time of the second campaign is: Don't cry because it's over, smile because it happened."
