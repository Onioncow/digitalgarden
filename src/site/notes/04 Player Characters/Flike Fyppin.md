## History
Born 17th Marpenoth 1481 (absolute) of the 2nd Era in his home town just east of [[05 World/Material Plane/Kingdom of Unurid/Cities/Gefestmere/Gefestmere\|Gefestmere]].

### Backstory
- From the forest near Gefestmere. His people didn't agree with gambling so he left home at the age of 8
- Once he left, he joined a church (cult) that worships [[02 Lore/Gods/Outer Gods/The Great Wheel\|The Great Wheel]] as a god of chance and gambling
- He learnt everything he knows about his religion (cult) from Habby
- He has a tabaxi uncle in the [[05 World/Material Plane/_Geography/Land/Condeus#Perfumed Quag\|Perfumed Quag]]
