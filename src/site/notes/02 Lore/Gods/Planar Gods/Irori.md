# Master of Masters
Irori exemplifies the concepts of self-perfection. His dogma states that he was a mortal who gained godhood through achieving a physical and mental state that surpassed mortal limitations. His followers seek to emulate their god’s divine state by perfecting themselves using the words of the _Unbinding of Fetters_, Irori’s sacred text. The illuminated pages of the tome detail numerous physical, spiritual, and mental exercises, as well as methods of learning and remembering.

**Edicts** be humble; help others perfect themselves; hone your body, mind, and spirit to a more perfect state; practice discipline  
**Anathema** engage in overly unhealthy or self-destructive behaviors, destroy an important historical text, repeatedly fail to maintain self-control  
**Areas of Concern** history, knowledge, and self-perfection  
**Religious Symbol** blue hand  
**Sacred Animal** snail  
**Sacred Color(s)** blue, white
