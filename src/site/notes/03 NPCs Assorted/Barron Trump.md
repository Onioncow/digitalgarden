## Description
A very tall human noble, standing at around 6'9. He has short blonde hair and wears the finest clothes. He carries a rapier for self defence and status, it is heavily ornamented with jewels and gold. He also carries his suitcase (he loves it) - it is a plain, but well crafted suitcase created by [[03 NPCs Assorted/Bayle Fyppin, the Dread\|Bayle Fyppin, the Dread]]'s own magic.

## Personality
He has attachment issues due to his childhood trauma. His adoptive father, [[03 NPCs Assorted/Donald Trump\|Donald Trump]], mistreated him so he now finds himself desperate to connect with others, becoming overly attached.

## Powers
Being hit with his suitcase causes mental damage

## Possessions
- His suitcase:
	- It is magical in nature
	- It appears to be able to contain more than should be physically possible, not to dissimilar to a bag of holding
