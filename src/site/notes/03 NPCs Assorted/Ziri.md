## History
### Hidden Ritual
Ziri stumbled upon the Chronos Ritual when she followed her shady coworkers, discovering a hidden cultists lair beneath the church in [[05 World/Material Plane/03 Cities/Gefestmere/Gefestmere\|Gefestmere]]. She decided to help those imprisoned by the cultists break free and set them up in a local tavern while she kept under cover. She then helped the party throughout their journey, risking her life in the process.

Once the dust had settled, she got to work rebuilding the Grand Cathedral.
